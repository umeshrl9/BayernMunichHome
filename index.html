The official website of Bayern Munich!

Bayern Munich is one of the most successful and popular football clubs in the world. Founded in 1900, the club has a storied history of dominance in German and European football. With a record number of Bundesliga titles and numerous DFB-Pokal victories, Bayern Munich has established itself as the premier club in Germany. Their success isn't limited to domestic competitions; Bayern has also triumphed in the UEFA Champions League multiple times, showcasing their prowess on the international stage. Known for their attacking style of play, Bayern Munich boasts a roster of world-class players and has been home to football legends like Franz Beckenbauer, Gerd MÃ¼ller, and Philipp Lahm. The club's home ground, the Allianz Arena, is an architectural marvel and a fortress for the team. Off the pitch, Bayern Munich is renowned for its strong fan base, financial stability, and commitment to community initiatives. The club's motto, "Mia san Mia," encapsulates their proud Bavarian identity and relentless pursuit of excellence. Whether you are a lifelong supporter or a new fan, Bayern Munich's rich history and bright future offer something for everyone to admire.
